Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI learn about my property?
Vailo AI scans your existing property guides, website content, and house rules. You can also manually upload PDF guides or link your booking platform for real-time data sync.
Can I integrate it with my current PMS?
Yes! Vailo supports major Property Management Systems. This allows the AI to know exactly who is checking in, their booking dates, and specific guest requirements.
Is there a free trial?
Absolutely. Our Starter plan is free forever for 1 property, so you can test the AI concierge features before scaling to our Basic or Pro plans.
Does it support multiple languages?
Yes, Vailo is fluent in over 25 languages. It automatically detects the guest's language and responds naturally in their native tongue.
